Lens Options

We offer a full range of prescription lens options to match your vision needs and lifestyle.

Single Vision

Corrects one field of vision — near, intermediate, or distance. Ideal for most prescriptions.

Bifocal Lenses

Two viewing zones: distance on top, reading at bottom, separated by a visible line.

Progressive Lenses

No-line multifocal lenses offering a gradual transition from distance to near vision.

Blue Light Filter

Coating that filters harmful high-energy blue light from screens and LEDs to reduce eye strain.

Anti-Reflective Coating

Reduces glare, reflections, and halos from headlights — ideal for night driving.

Photochromic (Transitions)

Lenses that automatically darken outdoors and return to clear indoors.
